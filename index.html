About Me

I'm Tcherno, originally from Portugal. I'm a huge fan of sports and arts. My journey began with football, and I always dreamed of becoming a professional player. It wasn't an easy dream to chase, the chances were slim due to my circumstances and health, including asthma. To make it even tougher, my dream wasn’t supported by my parents. But despite these challenges, I persevered. Against all odds, I had the chance to sign with a football club, a testament to my determination and resilienc, but... just 5 years later I experienced an ACL knee injury that required surgery and nearly a year of recovery. A tough experience that changed the course of my life. While recovering from an injury, my passion for dance grew stronger. A year later, I decided to learn how to dance, gradually transitioning into a self-taught freestyle dancer. While recovering from an injury, my passion for dance grew stronger. Like many of you, I started dancing for the pure joy of moving my body to the sound of my favorite track. It was all about having fun until other incredible dancers started to inspire me. A year later, I decided to learn how to dance, gradually transitioning into a self-taught freestyle dancer. In 2016, I decided to sacrifice a part of my life to raise my knowledge and skills in the world of dance. I left home, quit my job, sold all my belongings, and relocated to London, a city renowned for its vibrant art scene, creativity, and diversity... and the rest is history!
In 2020, the pandemic happened, an extremely challenging time for everyone. I lost my job and had to stop doing what I love due to the restrictions. It was the most difficult period for me, but that's when coaching came into my life. I made a conscious decision to shift my perspective from how the situation was affecting me to how it could improve me. I realized this shift in mindset is crucial for unlocking new perceptions of reality because difficult times are not meant to break you, but to make you stronger. During that time, I gained a deeper understanding of my strengths, weaknesses, values, and motivations. I focused on improving my skills and discovered new interests such as computer science, writing, and progressively, coaching. Looking back, it's a period I'm incredibly proud of. Not only did I gain valuable self-awareness, but I also planted the seeds for a fulfilling career path in coaching that leads me to continuous learning, self-awareness, and growth. My purpose now is to help others navigate their own challenges, unlock new perceptions, and break through limitations. I am dedicated to guiding people toward continuous learning, self-awareness, and growth.
I believe in you!
